We are a technology and innovation centre for aspiring tech entrepreneurs, enthusiasts and creative people to meet, learn, showcase their ideas and work together towards solving social and business needs using homegrown technology and solutions. Here, budding tech startups are empowered with the skills and tools to thrive and grow in a proper entrepreneurial ecosystem.

LearnFactory has also got a couple of works to show for locally; a website development project for the Abia state house of assembly, an e-registration platform currently in use by a chartered institute in Lagos, to mention but a few.

Perhaps the most interesting part of what LearnFactory is doing is the deployment of homegrown, young developers to these tasks. “We’ve been able to show that even though we are in Aba, our employees are still world class,” Chibueze explains. Developers at LearnFactory begin their journey by undergoing a free (not free initially) 3-month intensive bootcamp.
